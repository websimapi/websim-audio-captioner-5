How it works

1. Audio Upload: Upload any audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.) and our system will prepare it for processing.

2. AI Transcription: We use OpenAI's Whisper model (tiny.en variant) running entirely in your browser - no data is sent to external servers.

3. Word-Level Timing: The AI generates not just text, but precise timestamps for each word, enabling perfect synchronization with audio playback.

4. Real-Time Display: As you play the audio, words appear exactly when spoken, creating a natural captioning experience.

5. Download & Export: Export your captions as industry-standard SRT subtitle files for use in video editing or media players.

Technical Note: First-time usage downloads a ~40MB AI model to your browser. Subsequent uses are instant. All processing happens locally - your audio never leaves your device.
